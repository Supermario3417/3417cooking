Carne Asada

BANANA TIME! Start your day with vegetable covered pancakes!

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags :breakfast carrots food

instructions

step 1

Blend Milk, Seeds, and Mix Together until a thick batter is formed.

step 2

Pour 3/4 cups of batter into pan.

step 3

Flip as needed to cook sides, then remove from pan and serve.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk

tools

Blender

Pan

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
